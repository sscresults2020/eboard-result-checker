SSC, Dakhil and Vocational examinees across Bangladesh.

The SSC Result 2026 is the single most awaited announcement for Class 10 students in Bangladesh. This page tracks the SSC result 2026 published date and time, explains how to check your SSC result with marksheet using your roll and registration number, and lists the SMS codes for every board — Dhaka, Rajshahi, Chattogram, Cumilla, Jashore, Sylhet, Barishal, Dinajpur, Mymensingh, Madrasah and Technical.

Results are published by the education boards at 2:00 PM on announcement day, and both the online portals and the SMS service open at the same time. Keep your admit card and registration card beside you before you start.
